Finance Management Case Study

FMS stands for Finance Management System. It refers to software or platforms that help individuals or organizations manage their financial activities, such as tracking income, expenses, budgeting, and investments. The main goal is to provide a structured way to handle finances, ensure transparency, and improve financial decision-making.
In an FMS, users can record financial transactions, generate reports, monitor cash flows, and even set financial goals. It simplifies accounting and financial planning, making it easier to manage personal or business finances in real-time.

key pages (Members)



Onboarding page

The Admin Login Screen allows administrators to access the backend of the system, with fields for username/email, password, and optional multi-factor authentication for security. The Member Login Screen provides members access to their personal accounts, with fields for username/email, password, and options for social login or password recovery.

Dashboard page

The Member Dashboard provides a comprehensive financial overview, helping users manage their accounts efficiently. It prominently displays the Total Account Balance, giving users a quick snapshot of their overall funds. Below this, the Loan Balance section shows any outstanding loans, keeping members informed about their liabilities.
A Recent Transactions section lists the latest financial activities, ensuring users can track their spending and income at a glance. Additionally, the Upcoming Payments & Dues section highlights pending payments and deadlines, helping users stay on top of their financial commitments. The design is clean, user-friendly, and structured for easy navigation.

Transaction page

The Transactions Screen displays a list of deposit transactions, helping users track their financial activities. Each transaction entry includes the Deposit Amount, Deposit Type, Transaction Date, and Amount. A designated Action button allows users to view more details about each deposit. The layout is structured for clarity, with sorting and filtering options to help users quickly find specific transactions.
The Deposit Transaction Detail Page provides a detailed view of a selected deposit. It includes essential details such as Transaction ID, Deposit Amount, Deposit Type, Transaction Date, and Status. Additional information like payment method or remarks may also be displayed if applicable. This page ensures transparency and helps users verify their deposits efficiently.

Loan screens

The Loans Screen provides users with an overview of their active and past loans. It displays key details such as Loan Amount, Loan Type, Issued Date, Outstanding Balance, and Status. Users can quickly check their repayment progress and upcoming dues. An Action button allows access to more detailed loan information.
The Loan Details Screen presents a comprehensive breakdown of a specific loan. It includes Loan ID, Total Loan Amount, Loan Type, Issued Date, Interest Rate, Total Payable Amount, EMI Amount, Remaining Balance, and Payment Schedule. Users can also view past payments, due dates, and any applicable late fees. This screen ensures clarity in loan tracking and repayment management.

Loan Apply page

The Loan Apply Screen allows users to apply for different types of loans: Personal Loan, Education Loan, Gold Loan, and Cash Loan. Each loan type is presented as a button, and clicking on a specific loan type reveals its eligibility criteria, required documents, and terms & conditions in a card format.
Once the user selects a loan type and reviews the requirements, they can proceed to the Loan Application Form, where they enter necessary details such as loan amount, purpose, repayment period, and personal information before submitting their application. This step-by-step approach ensures clarity and a smooth application process.

Statements page

The Statements Page provides a comprehensive summary of all financial activities, including Deposits, Withdrawals, and Loan Repayments. Users can select a date range to view their monthly or custom-period statements for better financial tracking. Each statement entry includes details such as Transaction Date, Transaction Type (Deposit, Withdrawal, or Loan Repayment), Amount, Payment Method, and Status. Users can also download or export their statements in PDF or CSV format for record-keeping. To enhance accessibility, filters and search options allow users to quickly locate specific transactions, ensuring a seamless financial overview.

profile page

The Profile Screen provides users with access to their personal account details and settings. It displays essential profile information such as name, contact details, email, and account type. Users can edit their information using the Edit Profile option to update details as needed.
Additionally, the screen includes two key action buttons:
Account Closure Request – Allows users to initiate the process of closing their account.
Change Saving Amount – Enables users to modify their savings preferences.
