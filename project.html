Astrology-web Case Study

An Astrological Website Application provides users with astrological services, including personalized horoscope readings, Kundali generation, compatibility matching, and astrology consultations. Users can input their birth details to receive tailored predictions and explore insights into their life based on astrological principles. The platform may also offer features like daily or weekly horoscope updates, astrology-based matchmaking, and a dedicated section for consulting professional astrologers.

The Key Problem

  • Complex navigation structure confusing first-time users
  • Overwhelming astrological terminology with no explanations
  • High abandonment rate during Kundali matching process
  • Inaccessible design for elderly users

The Solution

  • Simplified user flow with guided navigation
  • Contextual help for complex terms
  • One-click access to common features
  • Large text and clear visual hierarchy

User Flow (Shop Page Experience)

Competitor Analysis

key pages



Homescreen page

The Home page serves as the main entry point for users, offering a quick overview of all astrology services and features. It includes highlights such as daily horoscopes, important astrological events,calendars and featured blogs and news. Users can also find shortcuts to essential sections like Kundali matching, astrologer consultations, and Panchanga. This page sets the tone for the platform, ensuring users can easily navigate to the services they need.

Horoscope page

The Horoscope section provides users with daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly horoscope readings based on their zodiac signs. These predictions are generated using planetary transits and astrological calculations, giving users insights into their future opportunities and challenges. Apart from general predictions, this section may also include personalized horoscope reports, allowing users to receive customized astrological guidance. This page serves as a daily reference for users who follow astrology for decision-making and personal growth.

services page

On the Services page, users will find a comprehensive list of astrology-related services offered, such as Kundali generation, Kundali matching, and astrologer consultations. Each service includes a brief description, allowing users to easily understand the offerings. Additionally, there is a section featuring the Top Experts available for consultations. This section highlights experienced astrologers with their specializations, allowing users to choose based on their specific needs (e.g., career advice, marriage compatibility). Below the services and experts, there’s a Testimonial section where users can read reviews from satisfied clients who have benefited from the services. This helps build trust and encourages new users to engage with the platform.

Shop-page and Product-Detail page

On the Shop page, users will first see filters on the left, allowing them to narrow down products based on categories . Below the filters, there are pricing options and a Recently Viewed section for quick access to previously browsed items. Additionally, the What's New section highlights the latest products added to the shop. The products are displayed in a grid layout, with options to sort by relevance, price, or new arrivals. Users can toggle between a grid and list view and hover over any product to see a 'Add to Cart' effect.

On the Product Detail page, users will find key information like stock availability, the product's location, and a detailed description of the item's astrological significance and uses. Beneath the details, there’s a section showcasing similar products that might interest the user, encouraging them to explore other items in the shop. This seamless layout ensures that users can make informed decisions and continue browsing with ease.

Blog and news page

TOn the Blog & News page, users will first see a section with Most Popular Blogs at the top, featuring the most-read and shared articles. \ Below that, there's a Featured Blogs section, highlighting expert-written articles on various astrological topics. The page also includes a search bar for users to easily find blogs on specific topics. Recent Blogs are displayed beneath, offering fresh insights on Vedic astrology, planetary movements, and remedies.

The News section is divided into multiple categories. At the top, there’s a News Highlights section covering both national and international astrology-related news. Below that, the Latest News section provides real-time updates on celestial events like planetary transits and eclipses. Finally, the Top Stories section showcases the most significant and impactful news that users should be aware of, ensuring they stay informed about the latest happenings in the world of astrology.
