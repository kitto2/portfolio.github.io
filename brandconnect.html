Brand-Connect Application- Case Study

BrandConnect is a white-label platform for brand owners and influencers to manage their brand, sell on Daraz, auto-post on social media, and engage with customers. It offers a three-step portfolio setup, customizable templates, and a subscription-based branded app launch.

The Key Problem

  • Small brands struggle to establish an online presence.
  • Manual posting is time-consuming.
  • Selling products across platforms is complex.
  • Lack of direct communication with audiences.
  • Developing a personal branded app is expensive.

The Solution

  • Helps brand owners showcase their work professionally.
  • Syncs social media for effortless content management.
  • Simplifies product listing and sales.
  • Enhances direct customer engagement.

key pages



Onboarding page

The Splash Screen is the first screen users see when they open the BrandConnect Influencer App, displaying the app logo and branding before transitioning to the login/signup page.
The Login/Signup Page allows existing users to enter their credentials and access the app, while new users can sign up using email or social login. After signing up, users proceed to the Onboarding Screens, where they choose between Brand Owner or Influencer, which determines their dashboard features. This step ensures a personalized experience based on their role before directing them to profile setup.

Profile Setup page

The Profile Setup consists of three steps, customized for Brand Owners and Influencers.
Brand Owners enter business details, connect social accounts, and choose a portfolio template to showcase their brand. Influencers provide personal details, link social platforms, and set up their content portfolio. Both complete setup before accessing their respective dashboards

Homepage page

The Home Screen displays a search bar for easy navigation, notifications for updates, Today’s Schedule showing planned posts and tasks, and a section for Recent Posts to quickly view and interact with the latest content. It serves as a central hub for managing daily activities and staying updated.

Schedule screens

The Schedule Tab helps users manage their content and communication efficiently. The Post Schedule section allows users to review and track upcoming or past posts, ensuring timely publishing. The Message Schedule section lets users schedule and check pre-written messages, automatically sending them on the set date. This streamlined scheduling ensures organized content planning and seamless audience engagement.

Shop page

The Shop Menu displays a categorized product list, featuring sections for on-sale items and promotional banners. Users can view, edit, or delete products, ensuring easy management of their storefront. This section helps Brand Owners organize and promote their offerings efficiently

chat & Notification page

The Chat & Notification Page, accessed via buttons on the Schedule Tab, allows seamless communication and updates. The Chat Page integrates multiple channels, displaying messages from each and enabling users to respond directly. The Notification Page provides real-time updates on important activities, ensuring users stay informed and engaged.

profile page

The Profile Page features an edit profile option, displays connected social media channels, and shows the user's followers at the top. It includes three main tabs: Portfolio for showcasing work, Settings for account preferences, and Analytics for tracking performance and engagement.
