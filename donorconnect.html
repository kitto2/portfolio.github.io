Donorconnect-App design
(Donation Management app)-- Case Study

DonorConnect is a donation management application designed to streamline the process of collecting, managing, and tracking donations for charitable causes. The app provides a platform where donors can easily contribute to various causes, track their donation history, and receive updates on the impact of their contributions. It aims to connect donors with organizations and projects that align with their values, making charitable giving more accessible, transparent, and efficient.
The app typically includes features like login and signup options, a homepage showcasing different causes, a request menu where users can submit donation requests, a favorites menu to save preferred causes, and a profile page to manage user details and donation history. The goal of DonorConnect is to create a seamless, user-friendly experience for both donors and organizations, helping to foster a community of giving and support.

The Key Problem

  • Hard to find nearby donations due to poor location filtering.
  • No trust in users (e.g., fake donors/requests).
  • Coordination happens outside the app (e.g., WhatsApp).
  • Old posts clutter the app (e.g., expired donations).

The Solution

  • Add location-based search (e.g., "Show donations within 5 km").
  • Verify users via phone/email and let donors/recipients rate each other.
  • Hide contact details until requests are accepted + add basic in-app messaging..
  • Auto-remove old posts after 30 days or let users mark items as "Claimed."

Competitor Analysis

key pages



Onboarding page

The onboarding screen offers a streamlined sign-up/login process. Legal agreements and notification opt-ins are placed below to avoid clutter. The login section supports phone/email entry, includes a “Remember Me” toggle, and quick links for password recovery or switching to sign-up.

Homescreen page

The homepage enables users to discover donation items through a search bar, category filters , and a "Recommendations" section. Each post previews the item type, description, owner’s name, and posting date, allowing quick browsing of nearby donations.
This screen provides complete donation information, including price (K150), category tags, detailed item description, owner profile, and a "Request" button to initiate contact. It centralizes all necessary details for users to assess and claim donations.

Donation request accepted page

Post-request acceptance, this screen provides a scannable QR code and the pickup address for item collection. Users can revisit the original post details via the "VIEW POST DETAIL" button, ensuring seamless coordination between donors and recipients.
Post Details screen displays comprehensive donation information, including the item category , detailed description, pickup address, and masked contact details. A "View in map" option helps users locate the item.

Create Post screens

This screen allows users to list items for donation. It starts with an option to upload a photo of the item, followed by fields for entering a title, description, and selecting the item’s condition . A second version adds category tags to improve discoverability. The process culminates with a prominent "Create Post" button, ensuring a straightforward and intuitive donation listing experience.

Saved/Bookmarks page

This page organizes all donation posts saved by the user into a centralized list. Each bookmarked item displays the donation title, category, owner name, and posting date for quick reference. Users can filter saved posts by category or search by keywords to locate specific items. A "Remove" option allows easy management of bookmarks, ensuring the list stays relevant and up-to-date. The design emphasizes simplicity and quick access to prioritized donations.

Requests page

This screen displays a list of active donation requests made by the user, organized under specific headers like "K150" with status indicators such as "Online" or "Supplies." Each request includes the item’s title, a short description, the requester’s name, and the date of the request. Entries are grouped by category or urgency, with instructional text clarifying request validity periods or usage rules. The layout emphasizes clarity, using consistent formatting for dates, requester details, and status tags to facilitate quick scanning and management of ongoing donation activities.

profile page

The profile screen showcases the user’s name, profile photo, and activity statistics for posts, followers, and following. An "Edit Profile" option allows updating personal details such as email, username, and address. A section lists the user’s donation posts with titles and engagement metrics. Navigation to settings includes account management, password changes, notification preferences, and privacy controls. Logout and app customization features are accessible at the bottom for seamless account management.
